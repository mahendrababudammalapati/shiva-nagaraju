|ABOUT
|SHIVA NAGARAJ

Kamepalli Shiva Nagaraj, born on February 19, 1990, hails from Pedakodamagundla in the Palnadu district of Andhra Pradesh. He completed his education up to the 10th grade at the Pedakodamagundla Government School. Later, he embarked on a journey in the film industry, moving to Hyderabad. Initially, he worked as an assistant director under the guidance of Mahendra, a seasoned film director. During this period, spanning two years, Shiva Nagaraj honed his skills in direction. Subsequently, he ventured into independent filmmaking, directing over 20 short films, 2 web series, and 3 private songs. Recently, he has embarked on a new project, venturing into the domain of theater-print movies. Shiva Nagaraj resides in Hyderabad, Telangana, along with Srinu, Rosemma, Venkateswarlu, and Ananthalakshmi.
